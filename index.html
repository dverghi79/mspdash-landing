The problem

ConnectWise has 400+ report templates. None of them answer "how did we do this week?"

01

Every Monday you export CSVs from ConnectWise, paste them into the same Excel spreadsheet, and rebuild the same charts. Utilization by tech, tickets by client, SLA breaches. It takes 30-45 minutes and you've been doing it for years.

02

BrightGauge costs $179/month and was built for 50+ tech MSPs with a dedicated ops person to configure dashboards. For a 5-person shop, that's a bigger line item than some of your monitoring tools, and you still have to set it up yourself.

03

Your techs close tickets but you have no idea if SLAs are slipping, which clients are eating margin, or whether utilization hit target this week. You find out at month-end when the invoice reconciliation reveals the damage.

How it works

Connect once. Get your Monday report. Drill into what matters.

Step 1

Connect your ConnectWise instance

Link your ConnectWise PSA with read-only API keys. MSPDash pulls tickets, time entries, agreements, and SLA data. Setup takes under 5 minutes.

Step 2

Get your Monday morning report

Every Monday at 7am, MSPDash emails you a clean summary: utilization by tech, SLA compliance, top revenue clients, agreement profitability, and tickets closed. No login needed.

Step 3

Drill into what matters

Open the dashboard to slice by technician, client, or date range. The views that matter to a small MSP are built in. No configuration required, no BI experience needed.
